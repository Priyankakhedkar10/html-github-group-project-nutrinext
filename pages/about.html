Key Aspects of a Life-Saving Healthy Lifestyle:

  • Diet and Nutrition:Consume a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while limiting saturated fats, sugar, and salt.
  • Physical Activity:Engage in at least 30-60 minutes of daily exercise to strengthen muscles, improve cardiovascular health, and maintain a healthy weight.
  • Disease Prevention:Avoid smoking, limit alcohol consumption, and maintain a healthy weight to reduce the risk of diabetes, stroke, and cardiovascular disease.
  • Mental Well-being:Manage stress through techniques like meditation or exercise, which helps prevent burnout and improves mood.
  • Rest and Hygiene: Ensure 7-9 hours of quality sleep nightly and practice good hygiene to support immune function.

DIET & NUTRITIONü•¨

Diet and nutrition involve the food you eat for energy, growth, and repair, emphasizing a balanced intake of macronutrients (carbs, protein, fats) and micronutrients (vitamins, minerals) from varied sources like whole grains, fruits, veggies, lean proteins, and healthy fats, while limiting added sugars, salt, and unhealthy fats for overall health and disease prevention

Key Components of a Healthy Diet

  • Carbohydrates: Primary energy source; focus on whole grains (oats, brown rice, whole wheat), fruits, vegetables, and pulses (beans, lentils).
  • Protein:Building blocks for tissues; from fish, poultry, beans, nuts, eggs, dairy, and lean meats.
  • Fats:Essential for absorption and function; prioritize unsaturated fats (avocado, nuts, seeds, olive oil) and limit trans fats.
  • Vitamins & Minerals:Crucial for immunity and bodily processes; found abundantly in fruits, vegetables, and whole foods.
  • Water:Essential for hydration; drink plenty of fluids daily.
